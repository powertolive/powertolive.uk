What We Need From You

We will consider applicants who have experience working in a supportive environment with evidence of aptitude and experience in compassionate supportive interactions.

Please also apply if you are a qualified Counsellor, Therapist or Clinical/Counselling Psychologist.

Alternatively you will be studying towards one of the following qualifications:

  • Diploma in Counselling
  • Degree or Masters in Counselling, Counselling/Clinical Psychology, Psychotherapy or Art Therapy
