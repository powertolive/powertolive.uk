Therapists

We want volunteer therapists with a background in counseling to join Power to Live.

We believe in practicing what we preach. We want our therapists to be willing to strive for awareness, courage and love in their lives even in adversity, so we can guide our clients to have the same skills.

Power to Live aims to build compassionate communities, so we want to offer a community for our therapists too, to access peer supervision and support, as well as social events.
